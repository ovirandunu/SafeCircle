### Common Questions Section

**Section Headline:** Common Questions

**FAQs:**

**Q1: How do I get started with SafeCircle?**
> Download the SafeCircle app from the App Store or Google Play, create your account, and follow the guided setup to connect your devices and add trusted contacts. The entire process typically takes 10-15 minutes.

**Q2: What happens during the learning period?**
> SafeCircle spends the first 7-10 days learning your normal routines and patterns. During this time, the system observes your device usage but won't send alerts to trusted contacts. You'll receive progress updates as the system learns your patterns.

**Q3: How do I respond to a check-in notification?**
> When you receive a check-in notification, simply open the app and tap "I'm Fine" to confirm you're okay. You can also call or text your trusted contacts directly. If you don't respond within the specified time window, your contacts will be notified.

**Q4: Can I pause monitoring temporarily?**
> Yes! You can enable "Away Mode" in the app when traveling or if your routine changes significantly. This temporarily adjusts monitoring parameters. You can also send a quick message to your trusted contacts explaining you'll be away.

**Q5: How do I change my trusted contacts?**
> Go to Settings > Trusted Contacts in the app. You can add, remove, or modify contacts at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and you can adjust each contact's notification preferences and access levels individually.

**Q6: What if I need help setting up a specific device?**
> Our support team can help! Email support@safecircle.com or use the in-app chat feature. Include your device model, and we'll provide step-by-step guidance. We also have video tutorials for most popular devices in the app.

## Notes for Review

### Audience-Specific Copy Differences

The main difference between the two landing pages is the **perspective and pain points**:

**Live Alone Page:**
- First-person perspective ("I want...")
- Focus on maintaining independence while being prepared
- Emphasis on control and agency

**Worry About Someone Page:**
- Third-person perspective about loved one ("They...")
- Focus on getting reassurance without being intrusive
- Emphasis on knowing they're safe without hovering

### Product Messaging Consistency

**Live Alone Version:**
- "Your trusted contacts know you're okay"
- "Help can reach you"

**Caregiver Version:**
- "You receive confirmation"
- "You're notified immediately"

### Key Messaging Themes Throughout

1. **Independence + Preparedness** (not either/or)
2. **Non-intrusive monitoring** (quiet, background, respectful)
3. **Peace of mind for both parties**
4. **No surveillance** (patterns only, not tracking)
